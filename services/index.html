SERVICES

Custom Branded

Promotional Products

Want to keep your company top of mind with clients? Bolster your sales team? Provide your prospects with useful items they’ll remember? Think about investing in customized promotional products.

Promotional products (also known as “swag”) can be defined as a tangible item imprinted with a company name, logo or message, designed to increase brand awareness and engagement on a physical level. Promotional products can be given to employees, customers, guests/visitors and prospective clients.

MGB has access to hundreds of high-quality products ranging from fun & entertaining to useful and productive -- all of them will make an impact for your brand. Our creative and cost-effective solutions will result in the perfect item for your program.

When we send you our customized proposal, it may include products from different categories in order to meet your objectives. This could include: bags, drinkware, office/desk, writing instruments, technology, PPE, health & wellness, auto, paper products, food/candy, golf/sport, kitchen/home, travel - literally anything that people use regularly can be branded and given out as a promotional product!

Corporate Apparel,

Uniforms & Team Wear

When you want to raise team spirit, add consistency, or make things more professional around the office, invest in corporate apparel.

We offer apparel items from headwear to socks, and everything in between including polos, t-shirts, sweatshirts, jackets, caps, toques. We also offer specialty clothing such as medical scrubs and safety/high visibility apparel.

We can personalize these items through a variety of decoration techniques, such as: embroidery, screen-printing, heat transfer, laser-etching or debossing. For those wanting name-brand merchandise, we have access to many great fashion and sports brands as well.

Employee Recognition

& Awards

Recognizing your employees is one of the most important things you can do for company morale.

We have a large selection of plaques, trophies, pins and executive gifts that can be personalized for years of service, sales achievements or thank you gifts. We can work with you to develop an ongoing employee recognition program or simply create a custom award for a unique celebratory event.
